# Post-Release Checkpoints

## T+30 minutes

- Confirm launch success rate is normal.
- Confirm no critical crash spike.
- Confirm persistence/save errors are not elevated.

## T+4 hours

- Re-check crash and startup trends.
- Confirm support channel signal is within expected range.
- Confirm no checksum/provenance distribution mismatch.

## T+24 hours

- Finalize release health status.
- Record follow-up issues and owner assignments.
- Mark release as stabilized or trigger remediation plan.
